job summary:

The Junior Data Scientist is a reliable independent contributor, providing strong data analysis. In addition to advanced analytical skills, this individual is also proficient at working with large, varied datasets. Junior Data Scientist is a curious, driven, and highly organized individual with a technical foundation of statistical, algorithmic and mining techniques. 
 
location: Charlotte, North Carolina
job type: Contract
salary: $23 - 25  per hour
work hours: 9am to 5pm
education: Bachelors
 
responsibilities:

•Apply Predictive and Prescriptive modeling techniques to various industry data
•Identify and refine additional reusable machine learning offerings
•Prepare proper validation and testing data sets
•Support real-time deployment and monitoring of the developed algorithms
•Design, analyze, and interpret the results of experiments
•Perform exploratory analyses to understand the performance of our machine learning systems
•Define, evaluate, dashboard and monitor key metrics, understanding root causes of changes
•Visualize data Insights and Machine Learning model results
  
qualifications:

•1-2 years of relevant quantitative and qualitative analytics experience
•Strong understanding and working knowledge of Classical Machine Learning Algorithms
•Data Visualization experience with Industry standard tools (such as Tableau, Power BI, Shiny)
•Data Science Coding skills (such as R, Python, or Java)
•Parallel Data-processing skills (such as Scala or PySpark)
•Strong working knowledge of class resampling techniques
•Experience with marketing analytics
•Proficiency with database technologies like SQL Server, MySQL or RedShift
•Excellent analytical, conceptual and problem-solving skills
•Strong communication skills
  
skills:
