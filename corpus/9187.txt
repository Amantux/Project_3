Description:

&bull; Develop, prototype and test predictive algorithms&nbsp;
&bull; Develop and implement data collection systems and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and data quality&nbsp;
&bull; Analyze data, interpret results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports&nbsp;

Qualifications:&nbsp;
&bull; Currently enrolled in a BS/MS program or equivalent in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Engineering, or related field&nbsp;
&bull; Proficiency in Python for data manipulation&nbsp;
&bull; Proficiency in Bash for data manipulation&nbsp;

&bull; Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy&nbsp;
&bull; Proficient in report writing and presenting findings&nbsp;
&bull; Open-minded&nbsp;
&bull; Amicable&nbsp;

This position can be part-time or full-time with flexible hours.
