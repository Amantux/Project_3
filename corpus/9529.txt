&bull; Acquire, merge, organize, rebuild, standardize, de-duplicate, verify, and structure data from multiple sources and maintain databases/data systems.&nbsp;
&bull; Filter, &ldquo;clean&rdquo;, and review medical data images&nbsp;
&bull; Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets&nbsp;

&bull; Analyze data, interpret results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports&nbsp;
