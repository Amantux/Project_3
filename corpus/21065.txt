Job Summary

The Office of Information Technology at UT Arlington provides premier solutions and support to our Maverick Community. We are a team of highly skilled professionals who approach our work with pride, respect, and a shared purpose. The Data Analyst performs complex work in requirements gathering, designing, building, and providing data sources and reports using specialized tools designed for data warehouse applications.
Essential Duties and Responsibilities

Interact with Business Analysts and Customers to identify Reporting Requirements. Work with Office of Information Technology to design and implement reporting data models to support Business Affairs. Design and Implement Customer facing reporting & dashboards. Evaluate & revise data models for accuracy & performance. Work with other reporting teams within the university to maintain design and data quality standards. Performs other duties as assigned.
