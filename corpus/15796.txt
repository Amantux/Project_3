Job Description
We are seeking a Sr. Scientist to join our team to design and conduct research and development studies. This person will also participate in decision making regarding research priorities for effective and efficient product development progression; analyze data and recommend further study direction.
The essential duties/responsibilities for this position are:
• Leads projects from early development, product transfer, manufacturing and clinical studies
• Participates in decision making regarding research priorities of the department and provides information on feasibility of prospective projects;
• Develops research plan, design experiments and outline research procedures to be followed;
• Identifies and provides training to subordinate personnel to carry out research;
• Investigates and develops new methods for solving complex technical problems;
• Plans research schedule according to timetable and establishes daily or weekly routines necessary to meet the timetable;
• Assists RA department for 510K submission;
• Conducts research using laboratory equipment, computer resources and libraries;
• Provides expertise in field of research to other department members;
• Provide knowledge and support of electrochemical biosensor development; and
• Performs and reports analysis of data to monitor.
Requirements of the position:
• Ph.D. in chemistry, biochemistry or related, or a combination of Bachelor’s degree and 6 years experience in a Scientist role in the IVD industry.
• 7-10 years of experience in assay development and improvement process in a quality system environment.
• Must have experience on biochemistry assay development, lipid panel assay development, immunoassay development and other related field
• Must be able to manage project and timelines effectively, organize work priorities and effectively interact with other team members;
• Knowledge of conducting clinical trial study and FDA 510K submission.
• Must have experience on biochemistry assay development, lipid panel assay development, immunoassay development and other related field.
• Must be able to use Excel program to present data and do basic statistical analysis.
