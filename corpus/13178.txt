Information about #12647 Sales & Operations Planning Sr Data Analyst

Work Location: GOJO Plaza, Akron OH
Role Purpose

• Responsible for advanced analytical models for operations and planning processes and systems.
• Develops data analytics and modeling for critical strategic projects representing Sales and Operations Planning (S&OP) team.
• Performs complex analyses.
Essential Functions and Responsibilities

• Creates supply chain/business models to deconstruct complex issues and processes.
• Queries company databases to extract key fulfillment data and synthesizes into meaningful data analysis models.
• Performs data collection and validation in order to construct analytical models used to assess scenario impact on Supply Chain cost to serve and service levels.
• Interfaces with cross-functional teams on business/data insights, trends and provides recommendations.
• Undertakes system analysis, designs and systems building activities together and in alignment with the S&OP team.
• Creates tools to allow for automated display of information (data transformed into actionable information).
• Collaborates with Information Technology and other business teams to create tools and processes ensuring data accuracy and integrity.
• Participates as a key member in a range of projects and tasks across the organization.
• Collaborates with Master Data Owners to maintain the integrity and quality of ERP Master Data.
• Responsible for MRP and Planning master data quality and integrity scorecards.
Education and Experience

Education/Experience:
• Minimum 5 (five) years experience in related ERP/MRP systems work environment, SAP is preferred.
• Strong background in S&OP, Master Scheduling, and Production Planning.
• Advanced Excel skills and data analysis/reporting tools, e.g. Power BI, Tableau.
• Minimum Bachelor Degree in Information Systems, Mathematics, Engineering, Finance, or a related discipline with relevant professional experience.
• APICS / ASCM certification (CPIM, CSCP) is preferred.

Soft skills:
• Excellent planning, mapping, and communication skill, being able to make in-the-moment decisions based on new information constantly.
• Work well under stress with the ability to deliver results on time.
• Must be self-directed, have excellent initiative and organization skills.
• Ability to work well with others and communicate with different levels of stakeholders in the organizations/upper management.
