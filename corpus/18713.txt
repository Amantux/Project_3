The Senior IT Disaster Recovery Engineer work assignments involve moderately complex to complex issues where the analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors.ResponsibilitiesThe Senior IT Disaster Recovery Engineer engages in all facets and activities of the DR Program and expected to:+ Implement policies and procedures for disaster recovery
