Senior Data Scientist

Avetta connects the worlds leading organizations with qualified suppliers, contractors, and vendors. We bring unmatched visibility to companies through cloud-based technology and human insight. As a result, we foster sustainable growth for businesses and their supply chains. Our SaaS subscription software is used by 85k+ active customers in over 100 countries.

Position Summary:

Reporting to VP of Analytics

Candidate will support Avetta's SaaS Analytics platform and our sales/marketing teams with insights gained by analyzing our company data in conjunction with external data

Job Responsibilities:
Work with stakeholders across the organization to identify analytical opportunities using company and external data
Mine company data to drive customer facing analytics solutions, internal process optimization and marketing analytics
Work with dev team to extract data from Avettas diverse databases to a common storage (data lake), cleanse/curate data as necessary, perform data exploratory analysis, build/validate models and work with team to put models into production
Develop processes to monitor model performance and ensure model is performing based on pre-defined metrics
Develop processes to explain model performance and key drivers to non-technical users
Identify project risks, communicate them to leadership/team and work with team to address risks in a timely manner
Mentor junior data scientists
Education/Experience:
B.S. in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science or equivalent
5-7 years of experience in applied data science. Candidate should be well versed in using various machine learning techniques (regression, decision trees, neural networks etc.) and understand when/why to use one or combination of techniques
Strong knowledge of programming languages such as Python (for data science) and SQL (for data munging)
Strong knowledge of visualization techniques to present results to stakeholders and clients
Experience using Jupyter notebooks for the analytics development environment
Experience working in AWS a plus
Ability to work effectively in a distributed, collaborative, agile environment and deliver solutions on a regular cadence
