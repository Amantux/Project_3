Job Overview
As a Director of Data Architecture, you will establish and lead the data architecture team which consists of data architects and data platform architects. You will be responsible for the overall state-of-the-art data platform design and data architecture for both customer facing and internal information systems, which is fully aligned with business strategy and capable of supporting monetization of our data. You will develop a strategy to improve the value of data, improve effectiveness of information stewardship, and streamline the flow of data within the organization and to our customers. The ideal candidate must have extensive background in data modeling, data integration, data management, modern data warehousing and advanced analytics, and data engineering practices and principles.
Job Responsibilities
- Lead and cultivate an environment that promotes learning; enabling resources to resolve and remove their own obstacles, while supporting individuals and teams to learn from failing fast.
- Collaborate with and exert influence across all cross functional teams including data engineering, Business Intelligence data science, and data integration teams
- Lead the data design of solutions for large projects and programs; through expert knowledge, translating high-level business requirements into data models and appropriate metadata, test data, and data standards.
- Lead and drive the design of conceptual, logical, and physical data models to support transactional and analytical systems.
- Lead and participate in the peer review and quality assurance of project architectural data artifacts; influence key architecture decisions and report implications to the architectural governance forums.
- Ensure that all relevant project teams follow a common set of data principles and patterns, utilize a standard set of technology frameworks, and apply data architecture best practices that help increase quality and agility.
- Provide thought leadership across the data community to ensure solutions are built in alignment with platform principles, design patterns, and standards.
- Develop data architectures that are inherently secure, robust, resilient, scalable, and enable API-centric and microservices ecosystem.
- Define data infrastructure and deployment topology by working with peers across the technology organization to support continuous integration and deployment, and DevOps processes.
- Identify emerging data technologies and develop architectural road-maps to modernize technology landscape and drive innovation.
- Attract, recruit, and retain capable individuals to build high-performance teams of data architects; establish mission and purpose for individuals and teams.
Required Education, Skills, and Qualifications
- Bachelors degree in computer science or related field, 12+ years in information technology and 5+ years data architecture experience across OLTP, reporting, analytics and/or data science
- Proven leadership experience in establishing and leading architecture and engineering organization in the data domain
- Prior work experience as a Data Architect, Data Modeler, Data Scientist, Data Analyst, or similar roles
- Extensive experience with enterprise data architecture for both transactional and analytical platforms, information life cycle management, master data management, data governance framework, data replication, data integration and large-scale data migration
- In-depth understanding of database structure and modeling principles
- Experience gathering and analyzing non-functional requirements and designing world class performing data solutions
- Experience with relational and non-relational data platforms, Data Lake concepts, modern ETL/ELT Automation Techniques, Data Cataloging, AI/ML usage in Analytics
- Demonstrable experience designing distributed data foundations and platforms in cloud-based and domain-driven microservice environment
- Familiarity with data-visualization and self-service BI/analytics tools
- Ability to design activities, and influence engineering and product directions
- Ability to interact with external DBA's, data engineers, product managers, business stakeholders, and technology leaders
Preferred Education, Skills, and Qualifications
- 5+ years of leadership experience building and leading architecture or data engineering organizations
- 5+ years of direct responsibility for strategy formulation and/or business analytics, database management, data analytics, data integration, and database development
- Experience in the FinTech space preferred
