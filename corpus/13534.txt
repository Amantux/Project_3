**Job Title:**



Data Scientist



**Job Description & Qualifications:**



The Dealer Analytics team in Credit Analytics is looking for a hardworking and motivated Data Scientist to work on dealer risk and sales initiatives within Ford Motor Company's Global Data, Insight, and Analytics (GDI&A) organization. In this role, you will perform analytics, segmentation and modeling work; build advanced models to support Ford Motor Credit Company's risk and sales activities; and report results in appropriate forums.



**Responsibilities:**



+ Utilize statistical software (e.g., SAS, Matlab, R) to develop models, forecasts, and optimization algorithms

+ Learn new software tools (e.g., Alteryx, Qlikview, ArcGIS, AnyLogic) as necessary

+ Collaborate with subject matter specialists to understand their requirements and business needs

+ Ensure overall quality of the data and solutions throughout the analytical development process

+ Fully understand the complex dealer data structure

+ Work with various data sources and platforms (e.g., PC, Hadoop, Teradata) to compile data

+ Execute both descriptive and inferential ad hoc requests in a timely manner

+ Work closely with business customers, IT, and other analytical teams within the GDI&A organization

+ Investigate data issues

+ Communicate and present models to business customers



**Basic Qualifications:**



+ Bachelor's Degree in a quantitative field such as Statistics, Economics, Applied Mathematics, Operations Research, Physics or a related field

+ 3+ years of experience in at least two of the following languages/tools: R, MATLAB, SQL, Python, and/or SAS



**Preferred Qualifications:**



+ Robust knowledge of statistical methods (i.e., logistic regression, time series, seasonal adjustments)

+ The ability to prioritize efforts, work independently, and progress multiple initiatives concurrently

+ Experience in and interest in learning new technologies

+ Experience in applications of operations research, mathematics, and/or statistics in an industrial/applied environment

+ Working experience, preferably in the automotive industry, with manipulating large scale data, performing analysis, and building statistical models

+ SAS proficiency or programming background

+ Linux and Windows experience

+ Strong oral and written communication skills

+ Ability to translate complex quantitative methods into business insights and present to a non-technical audience
