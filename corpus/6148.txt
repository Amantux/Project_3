Senior Data Scientist  
2 year contract role in Greensboro, NC. (on-sight only &ndash; no remote) for a Senior Data Scientist. Possible contract to hire for those interested in that as well. 

  Please note the contract here requires someone able to work without restriction in the US, and corp to corp is not allowed. So if not USC or GC and able to work directly with HTC you will not be eligible for this role.   
&nbsp; 
 Roles &amp; Responsibilities : 
Leading the process of designing and developing innovative, data-driven exploratory analysis and predictive and decision models to address business issues related to HealthCare/Semi-Conductor and value-based payment models. You will provide proactive, strategic consultative solutions related to program evaluation and independently collaborates and partners with management, project managers, and IT to develop, implement and manage an assigned project portfolio and will provide subject matter expertise, and assists with providing mentoring and training.


 THE IDEAL CANDIDATE WILL HAVE:   

Certification as a Certified Professional in Design Quality (CPHQ)
&bull;&nbsp;Thorough knowledge of statistical software, such as SAS/R/Python
&bull;&nbsp;Working knowledge of provider analytics
&bull;&nbsp;Working knowledge of SQL and of data visualization tools, such as Tableau
&bull; Experience working on Artificial Intelligence projects

 
Thorough Knowledge of: 
&bull;&nbsp;Predictive modeling, data mining, and risk adjustment
&bull;&nbsp;Statistical techniques and data science
&bull;&nbsp;Principles and practices of project management
&bull;&nbsp;Statistical software, such as SAS/R/Python
&bull;&nbsp;Windows based PC systems and Microsoft Word, Outlook, PowerPoint, and Excel, as well as report-generating software
&bull;&nbsp;Program outcomes evaluation metrics, such as National Committee for Quality Assurance (NCQA), Utilization Review Accreditation Commission (URAC), Agency for Healthcare/Semi-Conductor Research and Quality (AHRQ), and/or Leapfrog
&bull;&nbsp;Healthcare industry and healthcare information standards such as HL7, LOINC, FHIR, ICD 9/10 and CPT codes, industry standard groupers, such as ETGs, DRGs, and DCGs
&bull;&nbsp;Health plan and delivery system operations, healthcare informatics, and healthcare benefits and terminology

  
 Ability to: 
&bull;&nbsp;Perform new product development from concept to final delivery
&bull;&nbsp;Develop new algorithms and modify existing algorithms in order to solve business problems
&bull;&nbsp;Identify and analyze database and data processing problems and recommend solutions
&bull;&nbsp;Apply business acumen and quickly and effectively assess business situations, risks, and opportunities in order to ensure beneficial outcomes
&bull;&nbsp;Manage multiple projects simultaneously, organize work, maintain accurate records, and achieve goals and timelines
&bull;&nbsp;Develop data-driven and outcome-based initiatives to improve business decision making
&bull;&nbsp;Work collaboratively with individuals at all levels in a matrix organization while supporting multiple customers

 
 Education and Experience: 
&bull;&nbsp;Master&rsquo;s degree in Data Science, Predictive Analytics, Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science or a related field
&bull;&nbsp;Minimum of five years of advanced analytics experience in a healthcare/Semi-Conductor setting which included a minimum of three years of experience building and applying predictive models and performing programming using statistical software or an equivalent
