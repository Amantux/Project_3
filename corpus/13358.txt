Job Description
Ascendum Solutions is looking for a Data Scientist , who can do data science and data engineering and data analytics (need to be an expert on at least one topic and familiar with all). Flexibility is important to this role as our work is dynamic. Tools such as Python (including Pandas, NumPy and Scikit-learn) and Tensorflow will be heavily leveraged. Deep learning will be applied to images and videos (Convolutional Neural Networks) and potentially to longitudinal data (Recurrent Neural Networks).

Required Skills:

· Candidate must have worked with Python for: ETL/ELT, data science, machine learning, nlu\nlp projects

o Example of python libraries: pandas, nltk, textblob, scikit, spacy, flask, numpy, scipy, tensorflow, keras, genism, scrapy, selenium etc.

· Worked with big data technologies: Data Lake, Azure, DW, Hive, Vertica, MongoDB, ElasticSearch, Impala, Spark, HDFS, Redshift

At least 2 of the following:

· Has experience working as part of a data analytics project (SQL, T-SQL, PL/SQL, ETL\ELT)

· Worked with reporting tools (Tableau, PowerBI) to visualize the data

· Prepare data architecture/ data modeling – SQL and NoSQL, big data

· Experience with ML: nlu, nlp, clustering, linear regression, NN (rnn, ann, cnn et.), tuning training set, tuning existing model
