Please note this is a very math intensive opportunity; if you are extremely comfortable discussing and applying mathematical principles learned in undergraduate Calculus 1-3; including Differential Equations, Linear Algebra, and Statistics; then you may thrive in this role. Proficiency in a complied programming language: C/C++, Fortran, etc. Experience using Python, R, Java, or other computer languages to perform data science, develop automated solutions to process, and transform data.
