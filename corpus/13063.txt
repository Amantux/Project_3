Data Analyst/SQL Developer, St. Louis, MO

Analyst will be analyzing data in multiple databases, such as Oracle and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Big Query, Big Table, and Hive/Spark. Strong Data Analysis and SQL skills. Cloud experience, big plus, especially Google Big Query and Big Table. Must have the ability to look at a data model or data catalog and understand the relationships of table and be able to write SQL to examine the data. Must have the ability to write complex SQL. Willingness to learn new technologies and help troubleshoot and support Adobe marketing tools

The analyst will work with business users and team members to obtain business and technical requirements, such as new metric definition, report needs and exploratory data analysis. They will then need to apply strong data analysis and SQL skills to get the right answers from the data itself.

• Managing customer master data, through SQL, ETL, and reports.

• Provide quality assurance of customer data.

• Commissioning and decommissioning of marketing data sets.

• Processing confidential data and information according to guidelines.

• Helping develop reports and analysis.

• Managing and designing the reporting environment, including data sources, security, and metadata.

• Supporting initiatives for data integrity and normalization.

• Generating data sets and reports from multiple databases, including Oracle, Google Big Query and Big Table

• Troubleshooting the reporting database environment and reports.

• Evaluating changes and updates to source production systems.

• Training end users on new reports and dashboards.

• Providing technical expertise on data storage structures, data mining, and data cleansing.
