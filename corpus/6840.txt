The job The Sr. Data Scientist leads consumer insights analytic efforts through NLP/Speech analysis of structured and unstructured Voice of the Customer data, external sources of data, and operational data. This role will design and develop innovative and data-driven exploratory analysis as well as predictive and decision models to identify consumer insights and solve strategic advanced analytics priorities. The role will play a lead role in developing new algorithms and/or modify existing algorithms to solve complicated business problems. In addition, the role is required to experiment with different types of algorithms and models, analyze performances and identify best algorithms to employ.
