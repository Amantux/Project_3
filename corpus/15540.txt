Position

Scientist/ Senior Scientist- UV Coatings

Position Overview

An international cosmetics company is looking for a strong formulation scientist with an advanced degree in materials science, chemistry or engineering, who understands polymers, coatings, and UV chemistry processes. This position is a bench chemist position reporting to division VP. The position may have 1-2 direct reports. The scientist will develop and support testing and scale up of energy curable formulations. This new role will help support sales initiatives and interface with production, quality, regulatory and EHS teams.

Location

Gibbstown or Cherry Hill, New Jersey

Essential Job Functions

· Conduct research and development in a laboratory setting.

· Research and recommend new chemistries

· Research and recommend processing technology.

· Develop new testing protocols.

· Provide technical support for existing and new products regarding material selection, characterization and qualification, with ultimate translation to manufacturing.

· Derive requirements related to material properties and processing.

· Perform complex formulation, component and materials evaluations.

· Utilize advanced chemical knowledge, data, modeling and statistical tools to solve technical challenges.

· Support projects by performing laboratory work to improve product performance, troubleshoot and resolve customer issues.

· Knowledge of industry specific technologies and developing products.

· Ability to understand and apply statistical analysis.

· Ability to utilize multiple technologies.

· Proficient in MS Office applications.
