As a Data Scientist, you will be hands-on solving real world machine learning problems using Feature Labs and other open source tools for data science. As an internal user of our product, you will have the responsibility to provide feedback to the engineering team to guide future development.

This role also offers the opportunity to work directly with customers to help them make the most out of Feature Labs. This might involve writing tutorials, building custom demos, or traveling to support them onsite.

Feature Labs works with data from a number of different industries, so being able to quickly understand data, frame the the problem, and communicate the solution is required for success. A strong software engineering background will help you succeed in this role.

Requirements
BS or higher in Computer Science or equivalent
Experience doing data analysis in Python
Work in our Boston office near Copley Square
