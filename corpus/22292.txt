The Financial Operations Analyst will take an active role in reviewing both system design and transactional data to optimize Stamps.com’s business practices, working closely with third-party vendors and Stamps.com’s own development, product design, Business Intelligence, and Tier 2/3 technical support teams to ensure the reliability, accuracy, and profitability of our systems.
