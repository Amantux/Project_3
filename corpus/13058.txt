Overview

Our Personal Lines Actuarial department is seeking a Data Scientist to join our growing team in our Worcester, MA Corporate Headquarters.

As a Data Scientist, you will be part of a cross-functional team building and delivering innovative products. You will use Advanced Analytics methods along with your project management skills to assist in developing new approaches to complex business problems and thus make an impact on the company’s success. This is a high profile position that could involve partnering with leadership. You will need to have great communication skills where you can present complex technical topics in simple business language. This is a Full-time, Exempt role.
