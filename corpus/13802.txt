Data Scientist
New York, NY


Barclays Capital Inc.
What will you be doing?
• On behalf of global financial services firm, use SQL, Spark, and machine learning to develop natural language processing (NLP) rules to detect specific events from earnings call transcripts.
• Use optimization and simulation methods and tools to construct investment portfolio weights.
• Conduct investment analysis by utilizing events signals generated from earnings call transcripts.
• Develop machine learning models to better predict expected returns of bond/equity index components.
• Develop and explore new and existing datasets for application to investment research.
• Analyze large structured and unstructured datasets to define and evaluate problems, and recommend solutions.
• Participate in road mapping and planning data projects to build quantitative models for financial products, with a focus on agile development and iterative delivery.
• Evaluate data sources and providers for reliability, quality, and usefulness.
• Manipulate large structured and unstructured datasets for analysis, and provide signals to assist Trading desks.
What were looking for:
Requires Masters degree or foreign equivalent in Operations Research, Financial Engineering, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, or closely related quantitative field plus at least three (3) years of experience in position offered (Data Scientist) or in a role analyzing datasets and building quantitative models for financial products.
Full term (3 years) of experience must include: Using Python to build and validate statistical models for revenue projections; Analyzing datasets for interpreting machine learning models using open source Python packages; Performing coding using Python, Matlab, and SQL; Working with open source Machine Learning, open source natural language processing (NLP) frameworks, statistical analysis tools, and probability theory. FINRA Series 7 and 63 certifications must be obtained within six (6) months of hire.
