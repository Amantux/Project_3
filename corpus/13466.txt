JOB SUMMARY:

Extract and analyze health care data for specific studies
and corporate initiatives, assisting with data intensive projects. Serve as a technical resource to department
staff and management. Work with

their needs. Provide advice to management
regarding effective techniques to analyze and report on data to display the
most effective result.
