Job Description – Data Scientist

Nowigence, Inc. is currently looking for Data Scientists with deep machine learning experience, preferably in Charlotte, NC but with remote working as a choice for the right candidate.

Job responsibilities include:

· Build pipelines for multi-label classification on business articles

· Evaluate various algorithmic approaches in information retrieval and extraction

· Train and optimize predictive models

· Information retrieval (with web scraping and parsing)

· Maintain and sanitize entries in the databases

· Write wrappers to connect backend with frontend UI

Requirements:

· Proficient with Python

· Proficient with MongoDB and Flask

· Knowledge of NLP and machine learning libraries (e.g., NLTK, Scikit Learn, TensorFlow)

· Knowledge of common methods in information extraction

· Experience with tools and methods for feature engineering

· Experience in predictive modeling and parameter optimization

· Familiarity with VPS and Linux/Unix environments

· Strong research and problem-solving skills

· Excellent communication (written, verbal) and teamwork skills

Other Considerations: Salary and benefits will commensurate with experience.

Contact: If interested then please submit your resume with your cover letter toHR(at)nowigence.com

About Nowigence:

Nowigence, with advanced expertise in AI, is committed to increasing the speed to which users, teams, and businesses make data-driven decisions. Our AI Engine, Pluaris, comprehends data in real-time to deliver decisional boundaries and traceable intelligence derived from a rich spectrum of data types and sources, both inside and outside the business. This capability to deliver intelligence on demand, mimicking human comprehension, from a complex set of disparate sources applies to a wide variety of roles and processes. We serve Fortune enterprises and high-growth companies with over 200 employees, in digital transformation, data unification, and AI-based data comprehension.
