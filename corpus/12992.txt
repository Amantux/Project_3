The Laboratory Data Scientist must be familiar with data storage and standardization, database design and implementation, longitudinal data analysis, and carrying out statistical analysis to investigate data patterns. The Laboratory Data Scientist must have a depth of knowledge regarding molecular testing, especially next-generation sequencing, as well as working within an EMR/LIS.
