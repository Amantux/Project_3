The Business Intelligence and Data Analyst performs complex (journey-level) data analysis, solutions development, and reporting work for Strategic Initiatives. Works with internal and external teams to understand, interpret, and analyze various business operations, including the development and implementation of solutions that support continuous improvement and strategic planning across the agency. Works under general supervision, with moderate latitude for the use of initiative and independent judgment.
