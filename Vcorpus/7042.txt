Job Summary As Schneider tackles digital transformation and recognizes the value of aligning strategy, technology and processes with broader business goals, there's a growing need for experienced professionals who can think and deliver creative and innovative solutions to reduce complexity, establish solid technology and data management processes and ensure its consistent use across domains and capabilities.
