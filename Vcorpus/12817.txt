CALIBRE Systems Inc., an employee-owned Management Consulting and Digital Transformation company, is looking for a mid-level Data Analyst to join a team responsible for the statistical analysis and development of Cost Estimating Relationships (CER) equations for Army installations. They will explore new ways to analyze data using statistical methods, machine learning and predictive models.
