PURPOSE:
The Enterprise Portfolio Management Office (EPMO) is seeking an efficient, detail-oriented research analyst to join our growing organization. In this position, you will interpret, manage, and analyze organizational data in order to translate results into better solutions. You will be working with various source data systems and will be required to pull that data together, conduct validation and sanity checks, look for discrepancies, and organize the data in a way that permits effective analysis. Once the data is organized, the position will be required to investigate data trends and findings, leading to the development of visualizations and recommendations. The position will be tasked to work with information such as resource allocation and utilization data, project scheduling data, burn rates and other Agile metrics, defect metrics, and other associated project/program/portfolio information.

PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES:
Under the direction of the Manager, FEPOC Portfolio Management Office, the principle duties and responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

Duties and Responsibilities

Process Management

• Compliance
o Supports compliance of the standard PMO processes and ensures adherence to them.
o Understands and facilitates adherence to processes supporting PMO services.
• Stewardship
o Executes existing processes as written and provides feedback on potential opportunities for improvement
o Supports stewardship of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) by soliciting feedback from users and mentoring on how to follow the procedures.


Portfolio Management

• Oversight
o Supports oversight of the FEPOC PMO portfolio.
o Supports collection of portfolio metrics and preparation of reports and analyses
o Facilitates FEPOC PMO portfolio oversight activities to monitor and control portfolio performance.
• Strategy
o Awareness of corporate strategy.
• Reporting
o Assembles and validates pre-defined reports
o Provides verbal presentations of report content
o Designs and prepares simple reports
o Develops graphs and charts as appropriate to support reporting, including metrics, trends, anomalies, etc. represent very detailed data at a summary level.
o Supports and implements presentation formats and scenarios to help convey information to executives.
• Tools
o Maintains high quality of information within the PPM tool.

Data Analysis, Reporting, and Presentation

• Analysis
o Performs standard data analysis, draws conclusions, and makes recommendations for risk mitigation. Data includes: Schedule, Resource, Milestone, Issues, Risks, Dependencies, and Financials.
• Facilitation
o Contributes in portfolio review meetings, including capture of meeting minutes and tracking of action items to resolution

o Supports project financial reporting at the portfolio level and contributes to the analysis and escalation

o Supports preparation of resource management reporting and contributes to the analysis and escalation

o Supports management of PMO enterprise schedule/calendar.
o Supports milestone-level integrated schedule oversight.
