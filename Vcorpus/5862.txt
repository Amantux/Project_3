The Enterprise Technology & Tools sector provides enterprise data collection, monitoring, presentation and alerting for the IT assets of our customer. ETT is building the data science team to better understand, integrate and utilize the data collected on IT assets. 
Odyssey Systems is in need of a Data Scientist to develop the collection, cleaning, integration, quality control and availability of data. 
 RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES 
• Domains: Application development, support, and troubleshooting; Data integrations & quality assurance; API subject matter expert.
• Design, develop and support data gathering, cleansing & integration between applications.
• Develop advanced data analysis functionality.
• Maintain technical documentation and contribute to the education of system administrators regarding data flows in ISD/LL.
• Evaluate and make recommendations on tools and direction of Analytics & Informatics

 MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS 
• Must be a U.S. citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a secret clearance
• MS plus 3 years minimum experience, or BS plus 5 years minimum experience 
• Software/applications: Python, Agile, Javascript, RESTful API.
• Developing, debugging, and executing software in a Windows and Linux environment.
• Developing solutions around automation of Data and analytics.
• Specifying, developing, and verifying models and simulations.
• General understanding of IT terminology 
 PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS  
• Experience with Business Intelligence (BI) tools & industry.
• Experience in IT asset management & tracking.
• Experience in data management, visualizations, and best practices.
• Experience in agile development process, including JIRA and GIT.
• Experience with Master Data Management.
• Experience configuring and maintaining docker/container environments.
• Experience with JSON and relational databases.
• Experience with BigFix, Splunk, Solarwinds, R, SAP ABAP 
 WORKING LOCATIONS & ADDITIONAL INFO
