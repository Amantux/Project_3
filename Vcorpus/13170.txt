Phreesia is looking for an Associate Director of Data Science to lead our data science team! You will report to the Director of Strategy and Analytics, and will manage and mentor data scientists, providing them with technical and analytical expertise and helping to define the Life Sciences data science roadmap. You will also act as a thought leader in data science and machine learning for the entire company. You will be responsible for working with senior leadership to shape and execute the Life Sciences Department's long-term strategy for data science and transforming healthcare through the use of data.

You will inspire and mentor a team of data scientists, while harnessing the power of data science and big data technologies to continually improve our ability to make smart decisions. This is a huge opportunity to discover valuable insights for our clients and healthcare organizations across the nation. You are someone who loves solving complex problems and be committed to constant improvement, both for yourself and those around you.

What You Will Do:
Mentor and manage the data science team (approx. 6 members). Work cross-functionally with Data Science team to define and create analytic tools and approaches to solve healthcare and business problems, both for internal stakeholders and for our clients
Define and execute the data science team's roadmap
Support the Analytics and Insights team, in addition to other internal groups.
Create high-value data applications and insights using Phreesia's current and future data assets

Lead development of new methodologies and approaches through implementation of new technologies

Communicating complex analytics concepts in a clear and concise manner to leadership
