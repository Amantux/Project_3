Amgen is seeking a Data Scientist to be based in our Thousand Oaks, CA global headquarters, who will report into the Director of R&D Compliance Analytics & Insights. The focus of Compliance Analytics & Insights (CAI) team is to advance the utilization of data, analytics, artificial intelligence and automation to unlock actionable insights from data. CAI aims to accomplish this by supplying solutions that efficiently provide insights and intelligence for the Quality, Compliance, Audit, Learning & Performance (QCAL&P) function and broader R&D stakeholders. This includes ensuring data access, application of appropriate analysis methods to unlock information and meaningful visualization and interpretation of information to derive actionable insights. A core component of CAI is to ensure effectiveness of the Quality Management System by analyzing data generated out of the quality system (i.e., quality events) to identify weak signals that should be further investigated.
