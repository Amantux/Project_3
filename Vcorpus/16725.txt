Responsibilities:

• Be a thought leader and evangelist to drive data adoption and knowledge at all levels of the organization

• Develop an investment strategy to define, grow and maintain an exemplary data science organization and infrastructure for the business

• Collaborate with cross-functional partners in product, engineering, operations, finance, and marketing to define, build and deliver data roadmaps

• Ensure data best practices, data dictionary, data hygiene, and consistency of metrics throughout the organization

• Support and drive data-informed decision-making throughout the Organization including all business functions

• Build high performance, responsive teams of data scientists, analysts and data engineers that are able to gather and synthesize data quickly and accurately at scale

• Constantly look for strategic ways to expand the charter of the Data Science team beyond tactical business support and initiatives

• Determine how to leverage data science, machine learning, and other analytical techniques to offer actionable insights both internally & externally

• Participate heavily with executive team, help set business strategy, represent data science, initiate and debate key strategic topics and opportunities from team’s work, and drive bold high-impact decisions

• Effectively communicate at all levels of the organization including executives and board members

• Provide timely turnarounds on data requests for “one-off” activities, such as conversion rates, product usage anomalies etc.

• Implement a leveling and performance management system for Data Science as a whole, across the company

Skill Sets and knowledge required:

• Have strong technical domain know-how to coach and grow the data science team

• Ability to bring positive influence on culture and value for Zoom’s data practices

• Have a strong builder mentality, can bring order to chaos, and know how to effectively “bring people along”

• Ability to effectively challenge the status quo and plan insightful roadmaps

• Experience in a complex, cross-functional organization where your teams need to collaborate and integrate with other teams across many disciplines

• Passionate about innovating solutions, delight users, and inspire colleagues

• Strong strategic business acumen, change management, and continuous improvement champion

• Strong interpersonal skills and ability to influence through creating fellowship

• Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Minimum Qualifications:

• BS/BA degree or equivalent years of experience

• 15+ years’ experience in algorithmic engineering, data science, or machine learning

• 10+ years of experience in managing, and mentoring data science, data engineering, and analytics teams in a high scale technology company

• Deep technical background with preferred MS or PhD in mathematics, statistics, computer science or a related field
