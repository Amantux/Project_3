Data Analyst

Position Description

TNS has an exciting opportunity for a temporary data analyst working on our Call Guardian products! In this role you will:

•Practice of using Internet information and other media to conduct research and document trends in robocalling and call authentication.
•Practice of using Internet information, especially free information on websites, in researching consumer complaints (or lack thereof) of unwanted and illegal robocalls.
•Use writing skills to update technical and process documentation to be shared internally and with customers.
•Use writing skills to develop stories regarding robocalling practices and how to combat bad robocalls.
•Use analytical skills to assist QA teams in performing software testing functions.
•Use analytical skills to review data entries, and review performance indicators to review robocalling data.
•Design experiments leading to better understanding and ways to improve customer experience by focusing on wanted and unwanted robocalls.
•Conduct handset testing to compare various robocalling solutions available in the market.
•Evaluate new data sources to determine how they might be utilized by TNS in combating robocalls.
•Help trouble-shoot data issues and work closely with clients, data scientists, operations, customer care and engineering.
•Maintain “Buddy Book” instruction manual documenting job responsibilities and functions so that other team members could complete such tasks in an absence.
