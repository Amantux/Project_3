The Judge group is looking for an experienced Data Scientist!

 

SUMMARY DESCRIPTION: &nbsp; 
Under direction, this position:&nbsp; 
1.Leads the process of designing and developing innovative, data-driven exploratory analysis and predictive and decision models to address business issues related to healthcare management and value-based payment models;&nbsp; 
2.Provides proactive, strategic consultative solutions related to population health and program evaluation and independently collaborates and partners with management, project managers, and IT to develop, implement and manage an assigned project portfolio;&nbsp; 
3.Provides subject matter expertise, and assists with providing mentoring and training; and&nbsp; 
4.Performs other duties as assigned.&nbsp; 

 

DISTINGUISHING CHARACTERISTICS: &nbsp; 
The Data Scientist III is the advanced working level classification in the series and is distinguished from the lead and expert level Data Scientist IV by the latter’s responsibility for acting as a lead consultant in data science project design, performance of the most complex duties, advanced application of Alliance processes and policies and performance of lead duties, including assigning and monitoring work.&nbsp; 

 
DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:&nbsp; 
 1.Leads the process of designing and developing innovative, data-driven exploratory analysis and predictive and decision models to address business issues related to healthcare management and value-based payment models, with duties including but not limited to:&nbsp; 
•Developing new algorithms and modifying existing algorithms to address complicated business problems, such as risk prediction and stratification, provider benchmarking, program evaluations and ROI analysis;&nbsp; 
•Transforming business goals into hypotheses and tangible data mining goals;&nbsp; 
•Planning and executing experiments;&nbsp; 
•Driving innovation in modeling technology and processes;&nbsp; 
•Completing documentation, reports, white papers and technical and statistical assessments;&nbsp; 
•Analyzing large claim data sets in order to develop conclusions about data integrity, accuracy and general relationships and making related recommendations;&nbsp; 
•Anticipating, investigating and analyzing database and data processing issues in order to identify causes and recommend solutions;&nbsp; 
•Identifying business issues and challenges, forming hypotheses, planning and conducting interviews and whiteboard sessions and performing reporting and analysis to synthesize conclusions, develop solutions and make recommendations; Supporting data science projects prioritization and planning and conducting due diligence concerning business implications of planned solutions;&nbsp; 
•Identifying reporting and analytics improvement opportunities; and&nbsp; 
•Providing proactive consultative strategic solutions to customers.&nbsp; 

 
2.Provides proactive, strategic consultative solutions related to population health and program evaluation and independently collaborates and partners with management, project managers and IT to develop, implement and manage an assigned project portfolio, with duties including but not limited to:&nbsp; 
•Collaborating with other business stakeholders to define business problems and design solutions using advanced analytics;&nbsp; 
•Collecting business requirements and mapping complicated business problems into modeling tasks;&nbsp; 
•Leading multiple complex projects simultaneously;&nbsp; 
•Serving as liaison between teams of analysts and senior management and coordinating the efforts of multiple analysts on a project;&nbsp; 
•Engaging in direct dialogue with customers to achieve agreement on project outcomes;&nbsp; 
&nbsp;•Providing day-to-day consultation to business users and participating in and contributing to cross-functional project teams;&nbsp; 
•Responsible for managing assigned workflows and ensuring reliability, integrity and timeliness of end products;&nbsp; 
•Developing project plans and strategies and executing assigned project plans to deliver solutions;&nbsp; 
•Monitoring project progress towards objectives and tracking project activities to ensure critical milestones are met;&nbsp; 
•Minimizing exposure to risk and identifying, reporting and escalating risks to management appropriately;&nbsp; 
•Managing customer relations and communicating proactively with customers on project progress, including keeping customers updated and informed of any issues that may result in variations or delays;&nbsp; 
•Gathering information regarding customer expectations in order to develop goals and meet expectations;&nbsp; 
•Collaborating with senior analysts and supervisor and requesting review as needed to ensure reliability, integrity and timeliness of end products;&nbsp; 
•Summarizing findings to develop and propose appropriate solutions; and&nbsp; 
•Preparing and delivering formal presentations of work to customers.&nbsp; 

 
3.Provides subject matter expertise and assists with providing mentoring and training, with duties including but not limited to:&nbsp; 
•Performing as a data science liaison and providing organizational-wide support and expertise in data science;&nbsp; 
•Coaching and mentoring project team members, guiding them in making sound judgment calls and ensuring accountability for their assigned work;&nbsp; 
•Providing training and cross-training of end users and ATS staff related to areas of responsibility;&nbsp; 
•Reviewing processes and procedures to identify needs and providing recommendations to ensure continuous process improvement; and&nbsp; 
•Providing orientation, training, and technical direction to staff and project team members.&nbsp; 

 
4.Performs other duties as assigned.&nbsp; 

 
EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:&nbsp; 
•Master’s degree in Data Science, Predictive Analytics, Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science or a related field and a minimum of five (5) years of advanced analytics experience in a healthcare setting which included a minimum of three (3) years of experience building and applying predictive models and performing programming using statistical software or an equivalent combination of education and experience which would provide the required knowledge, skills and abilities may be qualifying.&nbsp; 
(Note: a Bachelor’s degree and two years of additional experience may substitute for the Master’s degree).&nbsp;
