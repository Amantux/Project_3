We are currently looking to hire a Data Scientist and a Junior Data Scientist to work out of our Buffalo, NY headquarters or to work remotely.

As a Data Scientist youll have the opportunity to work alongside Data Engineers and Software Engineers to solve business challenges by employing vast amounts of insurance underwriting data from 10s of millions of inspections and other data sources. A Data Scientist on our team will explore and understand structured and unstructured data sets, with an aim to design, train, and deploy machine learning models into a production software environment with a cloud-based infrastructure. The ideal candidate will have experience with and interest in collaborating with Software Engineers to deploy a reliable, customer-facing product. As our team is growing quickly, were keenly interested in candidates who understand how to design systems that scale and still know how to deliver results on a deadline when necessary.

Accountabilities:
• Participate in the complete development lifecycle of cloud-based Data Science products, from exploratory data analysis to deployment
• Identify new opportunities to improve customer experience using a largely untapped data warehouse of text, image, and columnar data
• Stay up to date on advancements in Natural Language Understanding and/or Computer Vision technologies, with a focus on tools that can have a real-world impact for our users
• Demonstrate model performance using visualizations and dashboards to define ongoing progress, compare model versions against key metrics, and provide a focus on our team objectives.
• Collaboratively give and receive feedback, including technical mentorship, by working with other Data Scientists via code review
• Work with other Data Scientists to develop reproducible models that predict and explain trends within a vast and heterogeneous data set
• Communicate with stakeholders outside of the Data Science team to answer specific business questions, understand customer pain-points, and identify opportunities for improvement
• Independently diagnose and remedy data hygiene issues to ensure reliable analysis and model development

Qualifications:
• 4+ years of professional experience
• Advanced degree (MS or PhD) in a technical field, e.g. Computer Science, Engineering, or Mathematics
• Proficiency in SQL and relational databases like Microsoft SQL Server or equivalent
• Experience training, deploying, monitoring, and maintaining reproducible machine learning models
• Advanced knowledge of unstructured data and machine learning technologies
• Proven experience with planning, execution, vision and success of complex technical projects
• Strong communication and technical skills

Competencies:
• Expertise in Python 3
• Thoroughly versed in statistics, linear algebra, and experimental design
• Strong knowledge of machine learning methods, including approaches that are neural, tree-based, etc.
• Experience with distributed version control systems (Git or equivalent) Experience with cloud-based platforms and services such as AWS, Azure, etc.
• Expertise in working with relational database management systems, including local or remote SQL DBs
• Working with larger than laptop-memory datasets with distributed computing technologies, e.g. Spark, Dask, Hadoop, Hive, etc.
• Aptitude for visualizing complex relationships though plots, diagrams, and dashboards
• Ability to prioritize use cases and remove/discern ambiguity
• Professional, accountable, and reliable with excellent organizational & time management skills
