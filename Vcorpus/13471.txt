Position Summary:

As a Data Scientist, you will work in our Information Solutions team focusing on solving real-world problems for customers, developing and using our Data Science & Analytics solution, and actively contributing to the development of our state-of-the-art preprocessing, modeling, and visualization capabilities. You will be responsible for designing and maintaining architectures to house data and performing descriptive statistics and analysis to develop insights, build models, and solve customer problems.

Position Requirements:
Active Clearance
Minimum of 6 years of experience with a BS/BA in Software Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or similar degree field.
4+ years of working experience in data science and demonstrated experience with SQL, Python and/or R development
Have full stack experience in data collection, aggregation, analysis, visualization, productization, and monitoring
Experience in multiple business intelligence platforms, data visualization, and analytics techniques
Git or equivalent
Desired Qualifications:
Experience with Cloud Services (AWS and/or MS Azure)
Experience with SaaS platform and product development
Experience developing web interfaces, developing/managing data warehousing solutions, dashboards, and scorecards
