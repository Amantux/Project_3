Discovery is looking for a Data Scientist to play a key role in modeling, data product innovation, and process automation in a data-driven advertising sales team. Reporting to the Data Science Lead, this individual would use statistical analysis, machine learning, and data engineering to drive business efficiencies and advance the development and enhancement of data products.
The Data Scientist role provides support to the Engage team on Discovery’s US networks which include: HGTV, ID, Animal Planet, DIY Network, Food Network, TLC, OWN, Cooking Channel, Discovery Channel, Travel Channel, Science & Motor Trend Network. The Engage team is Discovery’s data-driven linear tv and cross platform ad sales and analytics solution.
