Sonos

Manager (Former Employee) - Boston, MA - June 13, 2018
Very fast paced work environment yet bogged down by too many managerial decisions, not a lot of room for calling your own shots to do the right thing without kicking a hornets nest of critical opinions. Not a lot of career advancement opportunities. Complete lack of understanding from global leadership on how the rest of the company works, no understanding of necessary workforce requirements and headcount for the magnitude and responsibilities the job entails. With the most recent round of layoffs the company just made incredibly difficult jobs even more difficult by cutting manpower when it should be feeding the machine to make itself run smoother.

Pros

Great culture, product discounts, amazing people to work with

Cons

Short-sighted global leadership, lack of understanding of day-to-day stresses and requirements, lack of headcount, work-life balance totally out of whack
