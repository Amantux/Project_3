How is the work-life balance at Northeastern University?
Do people working at Northeastern University have the time and location flexibility they need? Indeeds Work Happiness survey asked over 160 current and former employees. Of the respondents, 49% said that they strongly agreed that they have the flexibility they need in terms of both time and location, and 21% said that they agreed.

Does Northeastern University let you work from home?
Indeeds survey asked over 150 current and former employees whether they could work remotely at Northeastern University. 67% said yes. When asked if they were satisfied with the work from home policy, 55% said no. 73% said that they did not receive an allowance for work from home costs at Northeastern University.

Is it stressful working at Northeastern University?
Indeeds Work Happiness survey asked over 81 current and former employees if they felt stressed working at Northeastern University. Of the respondents, 42% said that they strongly agreed or agreed that they felt stressed at work most of the time.

Is learning and development for employees important at Northeastern University?
Indeeds Work Happiness survey asked over 162 current and former employees whether they often learn something at work. Of the respondents, 64% said that they strongly agreed or agreed that they do.
