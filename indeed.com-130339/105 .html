Tread lightly

Maintenance Technician (Former Employee) - Latham, NY - March 6, 2022
In maintenance, they are generally hiring for a second shift position. Every supervisor on 2nd shift in different departments is fair and reasonable. Unfortunately most of the pushback you will get will be from the maintenance department. It is constantly understaffed. You will be working second shift by yourself. Their weak point is in finding competent electrical technicians. You will have to be electrically competent in order to excel at this position. This is not a place for a mechanic cutting their teeth in the trade. You need to know what you're doing going in. Pray that no one singles you out... especially in the maintenance department. If you have issues HR will not respond and will not help you out. You will be expected to carry the whole shift by yourself. It will seem as if people are working against you in your department... Not working with you. Good benefits and sign on bonus, though. Also plenty of time off and the company is very flexible. Happy when I got the job and even happier when I got tired of the nonsense and quit.
